THEIR PHILOSOPHY

At OCAFE we strive to make everything we do part of a holistically thoughtful and sustainable experience. From the products we bring you and where they come from, to our staff, to our relationship with our patrons and the community, we believe in creating a place that is like a second home.

At OCAFE we strive for quality and consistency, only working with the best ingredients and local vendors. We are big believers in buying local and supporting small farms. Our veggies and fruits are supplied from the Finger Lakes region and we visit the union square market weekly. Local is better for your health and the environment!

To make a great Americano, you must start with great espresso.
