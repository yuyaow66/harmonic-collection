Let's explore some cafe around the city

This new cafe is a tea house + cafe. Besides offering teas, they offers cakes, cookies and ice cream. You can also get tea for to-go. I tried their tea set, which includes a pot of tea, cookies, baked sweet potato, orange and I upgraded a snack to oolong basque cheesecake. I love their tea selections but for the price point, I expected more treats in the set (should have some savory bites). Also, basque cheesecake tasted good but the texture was a bit too dry for my liking. As for the interior/ decor, it feels tranquil. Perfect place for dating/ meeting with friends. It was 4.5/5 experience for me. I will be back to try more.
